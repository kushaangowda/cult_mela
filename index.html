CULTURALS MELA

Get ready for a week filled with fun and memories, for the Culturals Mela, organized by the Culturals Council of IIT Indore, is here!
You might imagine things will be limited by the circumstances of the pandemic. Still, here we focus instead on using this as an opportunity by bringing you experiences that would not be possible offline. Become a part of this grand exhibition, enjoy events performed by professionals from across the oceans and beyond the borders, all from the comfort of your very own home sweet home. An unforgettable fusion of Enthusiasm, Excitement, Enjoyment serves as the highlight of an otherwise rather dull time stuck in your home.
15+ events, talks, workshops, and much more, conducted by as many as 12 clubs, from the 19th to the 25th of April. Mark your calendars now, and be there to witness this all-encompassing spectacle!
